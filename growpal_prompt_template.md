# GrowPal Meta Ads Prompt Generator Template

To generate high-converting image prompts for GrowPal, use the following template. It ensures consistency with the brand's identity and value proposition.

---

## 1. Brand Identity Core (Constants)
*   **Color Palette:** Deep Obsidian (#0a0a0b), Emerald/Mint Accent (#6ee7b7), Muted Grey (#8a8a96), White (#e8e8ec).
*   **Typography:** Modern, bold Sans-serif (Inter).
*   **Aesthetic:** Tech-noir, Apple-esque minimalism, High-end SaaS/Fintech.
*   **Core Offer:** GrowPal Sprint (3 months, 0 zł management, min. 2k budget).

---

## 2. Prompt Formula
**Prompt Structure:** [Composition/Subject] + [Action/Dynamic Element] + [Brand Colors/Lighting] + [UI/Overlay Text] + [Style/Technical Specs]

---

## 3. Template Execution Examples (Copy-Paste Ready)

### A. The Tech/AI Focus (Efficiency)
**Template:** 3D render of [Device/Subject] floating in [Environment]. [Action: Glowing with AI energy]. Palette: [Brand Colors]. Text: "[Key Hook]". [Technical Specs].

**Prompt Example:** 
> 3D render of a futuristic 3D icon representing a bar chart made of emerald glass floating in a dark, minimalist space. Soft mint glow emanating from the base. Palette: Deep obsidian (#0a0a0b) and Mint green (#6ee7b7). Text Overlay: "AI-DRIVEN GROWTH. 0 ZŁ FEE." Professional Octane render, 8k, high contrast.

### B. The Social Proof/Results Focus (Trust)
**Template:** Split screen or layered composition. Left: [The Problem]. Right: [The GrowPal Result]. Lighting: [Dark to Bright]. Text: "[Comparison/Solution]".

**Prompt Example:** 
> Split screen image. Left side: A dark, blurry office desk with red "Low Sales" charts. Right side: A bright, clean desk with a laptop showing a GrowPal Weekly Report with a massive green "ROAS 500%" badge. Palette: GrowPal Emerald green accents. Text: "STOP GUESSING. START SCALING." Hyper-realistic photography style.

### C. The Urgency/Scarcity Focus (Action)
**Template:** Central subject is a [Timer/Counter/Card]. Metallic texture. [Action: Pulsing light]. Text: "[Urgency Hook]". Dark background.

**Prompt Example:** 
> A sleek, black metallic digital ticket floating in a void. On the ticket, glowing mint green text reads "GROWPAL SPRINT: ONLY 3 SPOTS LEFT". The number 3 is glowing intensely. Style: Cyber-minimalism. Lighting: Teal rim light. 8k resolution, cinematic look.

---

## 4. How to generate a new prompt:
1.  **Choose an angle:** (AI Tech / Results / Urgency).
2.  **Select the Subject:** (Phone, Laptop, Graph, 3D Icon, Card).
3.  **Insert the text:** (Must be in Inter Bold, Polish or English).
4.  **Enforce colors:** (Always specify #0a0a0b and #6ee7b7).
5.  **Add "Nano Banana Pro" flavor:** (Keywords: octane render, 8k, cinematic lighting, hyper-realistic).
